About this project

The pleasure of sharing…​

Dear Reader,

Understand, love, and share. You may think this refers to my shrink, Romeo & Juliet, and Saint-Martin, but in this instance it just summarises some passions of mine: learning and understanding stuff; marvelling at the world around me – people, nature, music, art in general; and sharing what I find interesting or worth admiring.

Social networks or blogs are great sharing tools, but not suitable if you want to share a bit more thinking. So this site has been designed to share good old papers and essays, in suitable formats for computers, phones, and tablets. Its main fuel is the selfish pleasure I take in writing and sharing. And the hope that it will give you, dear Reader, some food for thought, the pleasure that comes with discovery, and also a bit of fun. The price to pay: active reading for more than half a page. ;-)

The first section contains papers about some science-related topics I find particularly fascinating. I try to present them in an accessible way – and from a bit of a "different angle" as well. It is still in the making with more stuff in the pipeline, but we have to start somewhere: an introductory paper, then a 3-episode series about energy. To get started: click here!

Other sections will follow – music, art, society, etc. Just bear with me…​ or, better, contribute! If the temptation of writing teases you, you are most welcome to submit a paper. I will be very happy to publish it. The single publishing criterion is both simple and deliciously arbitrary: that I find the paper well written, interesting, and in the spirit described above! Just email me at my usual address or at pierrestuff20@gmail.com.

I hope you will enjoy the trip – I certainly do!

Many thanks for your visit.

Pierre Combelles
